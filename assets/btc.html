How to read this page

Net position

Aggregate BTC longs minus aggregate BTC shorts across currently open positions. It describes exposure—not intent.

Directional tilt

Net exposure divided by gross exposure. It shows direction while reducing the distortion of changing position size.

Conviction

A normalized directional measure. Larger absolute values indicate a stronger aggregate lean; they do not imply better odds.
