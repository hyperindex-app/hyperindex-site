The edge

What the dashboard is designed to answer

Other tools make you inspect wallets one by one. HyperIndex answers a narrower question: what are 100 screened directional traders doing as a group, and what changed enough to matter?

01 / CURATE

A deliberately screened set

A proprietary multi-signal process evaluates accounts across time and rejects activity that would create a misleading picture of directional conviction.

02 / DISTILL

The aggregate read

The HEAT Score summarizes the aggregate direction. Asset tilt, net leverage, conviction, and trader counts show where the agreement is concentrated.

03 / ALERT

Material-change monitoring

Core checks the full asset universe after every hourly refresh and sends a private Telegram alert for regime flips, meaningful net shifts, and conviction changes.

HyperIndex measures positioning, not future returns. The 100 can be early, wrong, hedged elsewhere, or caught in a squeeze. Use the read as context alongside price, funding, liquidity, and your own risk process.

Questions serious traders ask

How is this different from a whale tracker?

Whale trackers help you inspect individual wallets. HyperIndex maintains the same screened 100 and measures their aggregate positioning, reducing single-wallet noise and making regime changes easier to see.

Why pay when other Hyperliquid trackers are free?

Free trackers are useful for lookup, raw activity, and alerts you configure yourself. Core monitors the 100 for you: no wallet list to build, no rules to tune, and no interruption unless the aggregate state materially changes. It is designed to tell you when it is worth opening the other tools.

Is the data actually real-time?

The dashboard refreshes hourly. We say hourly because that is the current production cadence. Core evaluates the full universe immediately after each successful refresh.

How is Core different from free HyperIndex Signals?

Signals publishes two scheduled public snapshots per day. Core checks every tracked asset after each hourly refresh and privately interrupts you only when a regime, net exposure, tilt, or conviction change clears a materiality threshold. Same measurement system; much faster monitoring and broader coverage.

Does HyperIndex tell me what to trade?

No. It reports aggregated positioning and changes. It does not provide personalized recommendations, execute trades, connect to your wallet, or promise returns.

What if Core does not earn a place in my workflow?

Founding subscribers can try Core for seven days. If it is not useful, email support within seven calendar days of the first payment for a refund.
